About the Project

Where positioning
meets performance

This project analyzes how strategic product placement on retail shelves influences sales performance. Product positioning, shelf location, visibility, and store layout are critical factors that directly impact customer purchasing decisions and business revenue.

Using interactive Tableau dashboards, we examine real-world data to identify patterns between product placement and sales outcomes. The analysis helps retailers understand which placement strategies drive the highest returns and customer satisfaction.
