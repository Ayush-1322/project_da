📊 Strategic Retail Product Placement Analysis

An analytics project focused on understanding how product placement inside retail stores impacts sales performance. This project analyzes over 200 products across multiple store positions, traffic levels, customer demographics, and promotional strategies to identify the most effective placement strategies.

The goal is to help retailers optimize shelf placement, improve product visibility, and increase overall sales through data-driven insights.

🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

Analyze how store shelf positioning affects sales performance

Understand the impact of customer traffic levels on product visibility

Evaluate promotional and seasonal effects on product demand

Identify the best product placement strategies for different customer segments

Provide data-driven recommendations for retail store optimization

📈 Key Insights

🔹 End-cap placements generate higher product visibility and increased sales.

🔹 High-traffic areas significantly improve product performance compared to low-traffic zones.

🔹 Promotional campaigns boost sales across most product categories.

🔹 Seasonal products experience higher demand during peak shopping periods.

🔹 College students respond strongly to promotional offers compared to other demographics.

🔹 Front-of-store displays are effective for impulse purchases and promotional items.
